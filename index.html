About

Hannah Henson has been acting since the age of six and is ready to take on many more years of experience. She has studied Theatre at Bob Jones University and has been cast in several productions there.

If you are looking for a hardworking and passionate individual, here she is! Hannah is constantly learning everything she can about her craft and producing excellent performances in the process.
